Welcome to the demo of In Sheep's Clothing, a story driven security and social engineering awareness game.

You've just been hired at a Project Management SaaS startup called Flockly, and this week is your onboarding. Each day, you'll face a short workplace scenario that may seem harmless… or it may be a test of your security instincts.

In this demo, you'll:

  • Play through one simulated work week (5 daily scenarios)
  • Face security dilemmas with multiple choice outcomes
  • Learn to navigate tricky situations without throwing coworkers under the bus
  • Climb the ranks based on your answers, from Sheeple 🐑 to GOAT Guard 🐐
